# Cookie policy

**This site uses cookies and respects your privacy**

Cookies are small amounts of data saved in your browser to help identify the user. They for example enable websites to remember user settings and provide accurate reporting. 

Cookies can be set by the website or by third party software used by a website. Below is a summary of the types of cookies this website uses and why. 

### Website cookies

**Authentication**

This website uses cookies to store authentication data which enables customers who log-in to stay logged in throughout their session.

### Third part cookies

**Reporting**

This site uses Google Analytics third party software. This provides us with accurate reporting of site usage and it uses cookies to identify new vs returning customers among other things. This data is anonymised and contains on personal information that can identify the user. 

That’s all. You can delete these cookies from your web browser at any time.
